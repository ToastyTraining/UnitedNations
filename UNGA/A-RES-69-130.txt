Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 10 December 2014
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/69/514)]
69/130.	Observer status for the Pacific Community in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Pacific Community,
	1.	Decides to invite the Pacific Community to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the action necessary to implement the present resolution.

68th plenary meeting
10 December 2014
