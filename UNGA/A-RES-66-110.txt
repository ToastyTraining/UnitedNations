Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 9 December 2011
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/66/485)]
66/110.	Observer status for the International Renewable 
Energy Agency in the General Assembly
	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the International Renewable Energy Agency,
	1.	Decides to invite the International Renewable Energy Agency to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
