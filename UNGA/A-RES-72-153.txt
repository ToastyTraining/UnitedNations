		Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 19 December 2017


		[on the report of the Third Committee (A/72/434)]


	72/153.	Report of the Human Rights Council


	The General Assembly,
	Recalling its resolutions 60/251 of 15 March 2006, by which it established the Human Rights Council, and 65/281 of 17 June 2011 on the review of the Council,
	Recalling also its resolutions 62/219 of 22 December 2007, 63/160 of 18 December 2008, 64/143 of 18 December 2009, 65/195 of 21 December 2010, 66/136 of 19 December 2011, 67/151 of 20 December 2012, 68/144 of 18 December 2013, 69/155 of 18 December 2014, 70/136 of 17 December 2015 and 71/174 of 19 December 2016,
	Having considered the recommendations contained in the report of the Human Rights Council,1,2
	Takes note of the report of the Human Rights Council,1 including the addendum thereto,2 and its recommendations.

73rd plenary meeting 
19 December 2017

	1 	Official Records of the General Assembly, Seventy-second Session, Supplement No. 53 (A/72/53).
	2 	Ibid., Supplement No. 53A (A/72/53/Add.1).
