Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 13 December 2016
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/71/528)] 
71/154.	Observer status for the International Youth Organization for Ibero-America in the General Assembly 


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the International Youth Organization for Ibero-America,
	1.	Decides to invite the International Youth Organization for Ibero-America to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the action necessary to implement the present resolution.

62nd plenary meeting
