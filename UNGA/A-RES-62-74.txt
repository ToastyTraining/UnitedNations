Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 6 December 2007
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/62/461)]
62/74.	Observer status for the Italian-Latin American Institute in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Italian-Latin American Institute,
	1.	Decides to invite the Italian-Latin American Institute to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
