	RESOLUTION ADOPTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY

	[without reference to a Main Committee (A/48/L.1)]


48/2.Observer status for the Economic Cooperation Organization in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,

	Noting the desire of the Economic Cooperation Organization for cooperation between the United Nations and the Economic Cooperation Organization,

	1.	Decides to invite the Economic Cooperation Organization to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;

	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
