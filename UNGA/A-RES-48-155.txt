	RESOLUTION ADOPTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY

	[on the report of the Third Committee (A/48/633)]
	

		48/155.Situation of human rights in Estonia and Latvia


	The General Assembly,

	Recalling its resolution 47/115 of 16 December 1992,

	Taking into account the provisions of the Declaration on the Human Rights of Individuals Who are not Nationals of the Country in which They
Live, 1/

	Having considered the report of the Secretary-General, 2/

	1.	Takes note of the report of the Secretary-General and of the conclusions and recommendations therein of the United Nations fact-finding missions to Estonia and Latvia; 2/

	2.	Welcomes the cooperation that the Governments of Estonia and Latvia have extended to various international fact-finding missions;


	3.	Notes the existence of unresolved issues that involve large groups of population of different ethnic origin;



______________

	1/	Resolution 40/144, annex.

	2/	A/48/511.




	4.	Requests the Secretary-General to keep Member States informed of the situation of human rights in Estonia and Latvia, and decides to consider the question at one of its future sessions.
