Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 13 December 2016 
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/71/521)] 
71/157.	Observer status for the Central American Bank for Economic Integration in the General Assembly 


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Central American Bank for Economic Integration,
	1.	Decides to invite the Central American Bank for Economic Integration to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the action necessary to implement the present resolution.

62nd plenary meeting
