Agenda item 171



		Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 7 December 2017


		[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/72/474)]


	72/126.	Observer status for the ASEAN+3 Macroeconomic Research Office in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the ASEAN+3 Macroeconomic Research Office,
	1.	Decides to invite the ASEAN+3 Macroeconomic Research Office to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the action necessary to implement the present resolution.
