Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 11 December 2008
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/63/453)]
63/131.	Observer status for the South Centre in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the South Centre,
	1.	Decides to invite the South Centre to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
