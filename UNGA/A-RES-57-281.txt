Resolution adopted by the General Assembly
[on the report of the Fifth Committee (A/57/603)]
57/281.	Gratis personnel provided by Governments and other entities


	The General Assembly,
	Reaffirming its resolutions 51/243 of 15 September 1997, 52/234 of 26 June 1998, 53/11 of 26 October 1998 and 53/218 of 7 April 1999 and its decision 55/462 of 12 April 2001,
	Having considered the annual report of the Secretary-General on gratis personnel provided by Governments and other entities, covering the period from 1 January to 31 December 2001,1 and the related report of the Advisory Committee on Administrative and Budgetary Questions,2
	Takes note of the annual report of the Secretary-General.1

78th plenary meeting
20 December 2002
1 A/56/839.
2 See Official Records of the General Assembly, Fifty-sixth Session, Supplement No. 7A (A/56/7/Add.1-11), annex.
