	RESOLUTION ADOPTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY

	[without reference to a Main Committee (A/49/L.1 and Add.1)]


49/1.Observer status for the South Pacific Forum in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,

	Noting the desire of the South Pacific Forum for cooperation between the United Nations and the South Pacific Forum,

	1.	Decides to invite the South Pacific Forum to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;

	2.	Requests the Secretary General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
