Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 23 November 2005
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/60/521)]
		60/25.	Observer status for the Latin American Integration Association in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Latin American Integration Association,
	1.	Decides to invite the Latin American Integration Association to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
