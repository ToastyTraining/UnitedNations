Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 16 December 2013
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/68/478)]
68/121.	Observer status for the International Institute for the Unification of Private Law in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the International Institute for the Unification of Private Law,
	1.	Decides to invite the International Institute for the Unification of Private Law to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the action necessary to implement the present resolution.

68th plenary meeting
16 December 2013
