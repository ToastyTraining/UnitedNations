Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 14 December 2012
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/67/480)]
67/101.	Observer status for the Andean Development Corporation in the General Assembly
	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Andean Development Corporation,
	1.	Decides to invite the Andean Development Corporation to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.

56th plenary meeting
14 December 2012
