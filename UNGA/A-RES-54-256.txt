	RESOLUTION ADOPTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY

	[on the report of the Fifth Committee (A/54/511/Add.2)]


54/256.   Outsourcing practices in the United Nations

    The General Assembly,

    Having considered the report of the Office of Internal Oversight Services on the review of outsourcing practices at the United Nations,1 the report of the Joint Inspection Unit entitled "The challenge of outsourcing for the United Nations system"2 and the comments of the Administrative Committee on Coordination thereon,3 as well as the report of the Secretary-General on outsourcing practices in the United Nations4 and the related report of the Advisory Committee on Administrative and Budgetary Questions,5

    1.	Requests the Secretary-General to ensure that programme managers are guided by the basic reasons for outsourcing, as indicated in paragraph 4 of his report,4 and the goals, as indicated in paragraph 13 of the report;

    2.	Endorses the United Nations guidelines on outsourcing set out in the report of the Secretary-General,6 pending consideration of the report requested in paragraph 3 below;


    3.	Requests the Secretary-General to define, in a more detailed way and with justification, the criteria for decisions on which activities and services should or should not be outsourced, and to report thereon to the General Assembly at its fifty-fifth session.
