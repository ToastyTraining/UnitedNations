Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 19 October 2009
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/64/458)]
64/3.	Observer status for the International Olympic Committee in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the International Olympic Committee,
	1.	Decides to invite the International Olympic Committee to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
