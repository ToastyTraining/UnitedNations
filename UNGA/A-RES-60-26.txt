Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 23 November 2005
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/60/522)]
		60/26.	Observer status for the Common Fund for Commodities in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Common Fund for Commodities,
	1.	Decides to invite the Common Fund for Commodities to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.

53rd plenary meeting
