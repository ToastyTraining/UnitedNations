		Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 7 December 2017


		[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/72/475)]


	72/127.	Observer status for the Eurasian Group on Combating Money Laundering and Financing of Terrorism in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Eurasian Group on Combating Money Laundering and Financing of Terrorism,
	1.	Decides to invite the Eurasian Group on Combating Money Laundering and Financing of Terrorism to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the action necessary to implement the present resolution.
