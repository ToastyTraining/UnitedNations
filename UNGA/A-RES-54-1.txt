	RESOLUTION ADOPTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY

	[without reference to a Main Committee (A/54/L.1 and Add.1)]


54/1.	Admission of the Republic of Kiribati to membership in the United Nations

    The General Assembly,

    Having received the recommendation of the Security Council of 25 June 1999 that the Republic of Kiribati should be admitted to membership in the United Nations,1

    Having considered the application for membership of the Republic of Kiribati,2

    Decides to admit the Republic of Kiribati to membership in the United Nations.

	1st plenary meeting
	14 September 1999
