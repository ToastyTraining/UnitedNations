Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 16 December 2013
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/68/479)]
68/122.	Observer status for the International Anti-Corruption Academy in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the International Anti-Corruption Academy,
	1.	Decides to invite the International Anti-Corruption Academy to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the action necessary to implement the present resolution.

68th plenary meeting
16 December 2013
