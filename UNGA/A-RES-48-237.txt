	RESOLUTION ADOPTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY

	[without reference to a Main Committee (A/48/L.55)]


48/237.Observer status for the Commonwealth of Independent States in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,

	Noting the desire of the Commonwealth of Independent States to foster its cooperation with the United Nations,

	1.	Decides to invite the Commonwealth of Independent States to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;

	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
