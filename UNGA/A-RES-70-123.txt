Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 14 December 2015
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/70/534)]
70/123.	Observer status for the Indian Ocean Rim Association 
in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Indian Ocean Rim Association,
	1.	Decides to invite the Indian Ocean Rim Association to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the action necessary to implement the present resolution.

75th plenary meeting
14 December 2015
