	RESOLUTION ADOPTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY

	[without reference to a Main Committee (A/53/L.75 and Add.1)]


53/216.	Observer status for the Customs Cooperation Council in the General Assembly

    The General Assembly,

    Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Customs Cooperation Council,

    1.	Decides to invite the Customs Cooperation Council to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;

    2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.

	95th plenary meeting
