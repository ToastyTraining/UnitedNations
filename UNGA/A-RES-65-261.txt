Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 24 December 2010
[on the report of the Fifth Committee (A/65/645)]
65/261.	Procurement


	The General Assembly,
	Having considered the comprehensive report of the Secretary-General on United Nations procurement activities,1 and the addenda to the comprehensive report on procurement governance arrangements within the United Nations2 and on sustainable procurement,3 the related report of the Advisory Committee on Administrative and Budgetary Questions4 and the report of the Office of Internal Oversight Services on the audit of procurement management in the Secretariat,5
	Having also considered the reports of the Joint Inspection Unit on offshoring in United Nations system organizations6 and on the environmental profile of the United Nations system of organizations,7 as well as the notes by the Secretary-General transmitting his comments and those of the United Nations System Chief Executives Board for Coordination thereon,8
	1.	Requests the Secretary-General to submit an updated comprehensive report on United Nations procurement activities for consideration by the General Assembly at the first part of its resumed sixty-seventh session;
	2.	Decides to consider further the reports referenced in the present resolution at the first part of its resumed sixty-seventh session.

73rd plenary meeting
