54/3.	Admission of the Kingdom of Tonga to membership in the United Nations 

    The General Assembly,

    Having received the recommendation of the Security Council of 28 July 1999 that the Kingdom of Tonga should be admitted to membership in the United Nations,1

    Having considered the application for membership of the Kingdom of Tonga,2

    Decides to admit the Kingdom of Tonga to membership in the United Nations.

	1st plenary meeting
	14 September 1999
