Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 28 March 2007
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/61/462/Add.1)]
61/259.	Observer status for the Islamic Development Bank Group in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Islamic Development Bank Group,
	1.	Decides to invite the Islamic Development Bank Group to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
