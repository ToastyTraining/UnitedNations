Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 16 December 2009
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/64/457)]
64/122.	Observer status for the Global Fund to Fight AIDS, Tuberculosis and Malaria in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Global Fund to Fight AIDS, Tuberculosis and Malaria,
	1.	Decides to invite the Global Fund to Fight AIDS, Tuberculosis and Malaria to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
