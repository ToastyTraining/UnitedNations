Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 17 December 2007
[without reference to a Main Committee (A/62/L.33)]
62/97.	Graduation of Samoa


	The General Assembly,
	Recalling Economic and Social Council resolution 2007/35 of 27 July 2007 on the report of the Committee for Development Policy on its eighth session,
	Taking into account its resolution 59/209 of 20 December 2004 on a smooth transition strategy for countries graduating from the list of least developed countries,
	Takes note of the decision of the Economic and Social Council to endorse the recommendation of the Committee for Development Policy that Samoa be graduated from the list of least developed countries.1

74th plenary meeting
17 December 2007
1 Official Records of the Economic and Social Council, 2006, Supplement No. 13 (E/2006/33), chap. I, sect. A, recommendation 2.
