		57/3.	Admission of the Democratic Republic of Timor-Leste to membership in the United Nations


	The General Assembly,
	Having received the recommendation of the Security Council of 23 May 2002 that the Democratic Republic of Timor-Leste should be admitted to membership in the United Nations,1
	Having considered the application for membership of the Democratic Republic of Timor-Leste,2
	Decides to admit the Democratic Republic of Timor-Leste to membership in the United Nations.
20th plenary meeting
27 September 2002
1 A/57/258.
2 A/56/953-S/2002/558, annex.
