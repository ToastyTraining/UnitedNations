Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 9 December 2011
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/66/488)]
66/112.	Observer status for the Intergovernmental Authority 
on Development in the General Assembly
	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Intergovernmental Authority on Development,
	1.	Decides to invite the Intergovernmental Authority on Development to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
