	RESOLUTION ADOPTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY

	[without reference to a Main Committee (A/49/L.58 and Add.1)]


49/63.Admission of the Republic of Palau to membership in the United Nations


	The General Assembly,

	Having received the recommendation of the Security Council of 29 November 1994 that the Republic of Palau should be admitted to membership in the United Nations, 1/

	Having considered the application for membership of the Republic of Palau, 2/

	Decides to admit the Republic of Palau to membership in the United Nations.

	89th plenary meeting
	15 December 1994
