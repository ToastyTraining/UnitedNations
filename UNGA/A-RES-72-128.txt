		Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 7 December 2017


		[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/72/468)]


	72/128.	Observer status for the Fund for the Development of the Indigenous Peoples of Latin America and the Caribbean in the General Assembly 


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Fund for the Development of the Indigenous Peoples of Latin America and the Caribbean,
	1.	Decides to invite the Fund for the Development of the Indigenous Peoples of Latin America and the Caribbean to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the action necessary to implement the present resolution.
