	RESOLUTION ADOPTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY

	[without reference to a Main Committee (A/48/L.6 and Add.1)]


48/5.Observer status for the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,

	Noting the desire of the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe to intensify its cooperation with the United Nations,

	1.	Decides to invite the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;

	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
