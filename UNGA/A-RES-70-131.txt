Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 17 December 2015
[on the report of the Third Committee (A/70/482)]
70/131.	Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Discrimination against Women


	The General Assembly,
	Recalling its resolution 68/138 of 18 December 2013,
	1.	Welcomes the report of the Secretary-General on the status of the Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Discrimination against Women;1
	2.	Also welcomes the report of the Committee on the Elimination of Discrimination against Women on its fifty-eighth to sixtieth sessions;2
	3.	Invites the Chair of the Committee to address and to engage in an interactive dialogue with the General Assembly at its seventy-first and seventy-second sessions under the item on the advancement of women;
	4.	Requests the Secretary-General to submit to the General Assembly at its seventy-second session a report on the status of the Convention.

80th plenary meeting
17 December 2015

1 A/70/124. 
2 Official Records of the General Assembly, Seventieth Session, Supplement No. 38 (A/70/38).
