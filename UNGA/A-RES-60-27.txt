Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 23 November 2005
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/60/533)]
		60/27.	Observer status for the Hague Conference on Private 
International Law in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Hague Conference on Private International Law,
	1.	Decides to invite the Hague Conference on Private International Law to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer; 
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.

53rd plenary meeting
