	RESOLUTION ADOPTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY

	[without reference to a Main Committee (A/51/L.66)]


51/200.Appointment of the Secretary-General of the United Nations


	The General Assembly,

	Having considered the recommendation contained in Security Council resolution 1090 (1996) of 13 December 1996,1 

	Appoints Mr. Kofi Annan Secretary-General of the United Nations for a term of office beginning on 1 January 1997 and ending on 31 December 2001.

	88th plenary meeting
	17 December 1996
