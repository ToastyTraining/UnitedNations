	[without reference to a Main Committee (A/53/L.4 and Add.1)]


53/6.Observer status for the Organisation for Economic Cooperation and Development in the General Assembly

	The General Assembly,

	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Organisation for Economic Cooperation and Development,

	1.	Decides to invite the Organisation for Economic Cooperation and Development to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;

	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
