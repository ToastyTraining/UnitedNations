		Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 7 December 2017


		[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/72/473)]


	72/125.	Observer status for the International Network for Bamboo and Rattan in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the International Network for Bamboo and Rattan,
	1.	Decides to invite the International Network for Bamboo and Rattan to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the action necessary to implement the present resolution.
