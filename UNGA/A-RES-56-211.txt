Resolution adopted by the General Assembly
[on the report of the Second Committee (A/56/571)]
		56/211.	Integrated and coordinated implementation of and follow-up to the outcomes of the major United Nations conferences and summits in the economic and social fields 


	The General Assembly,
	Welcoming Economic and Social Council resolution 2001/21 of 26 July 2001,
	1.	Decides to examine how best to address the reviews of the implementation of the outcomes of the major United Nations conferences and summits of the 1990s, in the economic and social fields, including their format and periodicity;
	2.	Also decides to include in the provisional agenda of its fifty-seventh session an item entitled "Integrated and coordinated implementation of and follow-up to the outcomes of the major United Nations conferences and summits in the economic and social fields";
	3.	Requests the Secretary-General to make available to the Assembly the report requested by the Economic and Social Council for consideration at its substantive session of 2002 on the implementation of Council resolution 2001/21.

90th plenary meeting
21 December 2001
