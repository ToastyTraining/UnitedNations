Resolution adopted by the General Assembly on 6 December 2007
[on the report of the Sixth Committee (A/62/462)]
62/75.	Observer status for the Energy Charter Conference 
in the General Assembly


	The General Assembly,
	Wishing to promote cooperation between the United Nations and the Energy Charter Conference,
	1.	Decides to invite the Energy Charter Conference to participate in the sessions and the work of the General Assembly in the capacity of observer;
	2.	Requests the Secretary-General to take the necessary action to implement the present resolution.
