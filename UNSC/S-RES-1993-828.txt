	RESOLUTION 828 (1993)

	Adopted by the Security Council at its 3218th meeting,
	on 26 May 1993


	The Security Council,

	Having examined the application of Eritrea for admission to the United Nations (S/25793),

	Recommends to the General Assembly that Eritrea be admitted to membership in the United Nations.
